I am an Electrical Engineering PhD student at Stanford University advised by Professor Kunle Olukotun. My area of interest lies in computer architecture, compilers, and programming models. Recently, I am working on a Domain-specific Architectures and Compilers for dataflow accelerators.

I received my B.S. from Seoul National University, where I was fortunate enough to be advised by Professor Jae W. Lee and Jung Ho Ahn on designing hardware accelerators for domains such as Large Language Model training and Fully Homomorphic Encryption.
